At the Table with Mary Church Terrell Mollie Mrs. Terrell Mary Church

At The Table is a creative ideation tool for artists to break creative blocks by directly engaging with the words of civil rights activist and suffragist Mary Church Terrell (1863-1954). Thousands of creative prompts (questions and commands) have been generated by analyzing Terrell's writing from thousands of documents consisting of diaries, correspondence, speeches, publications, and poetry. These prompts not only encourage creative thinking, but act as entry points into Terrell's complex and multi-faceted life and career.
